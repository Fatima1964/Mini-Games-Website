Welcome to Hangman Challenge

Are you ready to test your word-guessing skills and unravel hidden words? In this thrilling game, you have the chance to prove your word mastery. Here's how it works:

How to Play:

  • Guess the word one letter at a time.
  • You have a limited number of incorrect guesses, so choose wisely.
  • Feeling stuck? Use the "Hint" button for a helpful nudge.
  • Feeling bold? Try the "Guess a Vowel" button for an instant vowel advantage.

Remember:

  • Hints and vowel guesses are limited, so use them strategically.
  • Victory awaits the word detective who can uncover the hidden secrets!

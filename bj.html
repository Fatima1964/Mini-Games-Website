How to Play?


1.Click on a number button to select your number.
2.A random number will be generated for the dealer.
3.Your selected number and the dealer's random number will be added to your total score.
4.If your total score equals 21, you win the game.
5.If your total score exceeds 21, you lose the game.
6.If your total score is less than 21, you can continue playing by selecting another number.

Replay?

After each game, you can click the "playAgain" button to start a new round.

Enjoy your mini blackjack game and have fun!
